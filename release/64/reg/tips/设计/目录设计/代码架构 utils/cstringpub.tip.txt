字符串处理
middleware/utils/cstringpub/

支持的功能有：
1.报文内容转字符串,示例:msg:
2.报文长度+报文内容转字符串，示例:[len:%u],msg:
3.最大支持报文长度为1024，超过后提示"out of range"
4.常见字符串定义，例如ERROR,WARNING,INFO
5.



==4.
#define STR_OUTOFRANGE "out of range"
#define STR_INVALIDLEN "invalid length"
#define STR_ERROR "error"
#define STR_WARNING "warning"
#define STR_INFO "info"
